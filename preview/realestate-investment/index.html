8 Hudson caters to all that life demands. That’s why we partnered with the highly trained fitness and spa experts at American Leisure to administer our impressive collection of lifestyle amenities. Residents of the condominium tower will enter from Hudson street into a dramatic 22-foot-high lobby that echoes the building’s exterior glass curtain wall. A navy blue stainless steel sculptural element houses the concierge while acting as a modern centerpiece for the lobby. Other materials include blue stone floors and a curved teak slatted wall.

Dramatic floor-to-ceiling windows create sun-drenched homes and wide open views of Manhattan skyline and the statue of Liberty. The distinctive open kitchens are designed for entertaining. Their sophisticated contemporary design includes a sleek steel vent hood, glass and stainless steel upper cabinets, cantilevered open shelves, stone counter tops and full height back splashes, stone-clad islands for dining, and elegant custom designed signature faucets. the cabinets, designed and fabricated in italy by dada, are quality crafted with integrated lighting and European hardware.
